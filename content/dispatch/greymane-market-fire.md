**VAELTHIR** — Emergency crews responded to a reported structure fire at the Greymane Market Hall in the Lower Trades District early this morning. Smoke was visible from several adjacent streets beginning around the fourth hour.

The Office of Resource and Civil Management issued a brief statement confirming that the situation is under control and that no fatalities have been reported at this time. The cause of the fire has not been disclosed. Merchants operating from the eastern wing of the hall have been asked to vacate until further notice.

An ORCM spokesperson declined to confirm whether the fire originated in a storage area or an active stall, stating only that "the relevant authorities are conducting a thorough assessment."

Foot traffic in the Lower Trades District has been redirected through Fenwick Lane. Residents are advised to avoid the area until the cordon is lifted.

No additional information has been provided. The Chronicle will update this report as details become available.

*Updated: Fourth hour, 37 minutes. No new information has been released.*

*Updated: Fifth hour, 12 minutes. ORCM confirms structural damage is "limited to one section." No further comment.*

*Updated: Sixth hour. An ORCM official was seen entering the Market Hall. No statement has been issued.*
