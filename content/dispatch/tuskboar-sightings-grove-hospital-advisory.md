Visitors to Grove Hospital are forewarned that tuskboars have been sighted on the hospital grounds. Hospital administration emphasizes that all services, appointments, and visiting hours are continuing as normal. Those traveling to the hospital are advised to keep to the main road, particularly after dark. Any visitor who feels unsafe making their way inside may call ahead to the front desk, and hospital security will meet them and escort them in. Additional security personnel have been posted around the grounds in the meantime. Updates will follow if conditions change.
