To every reader who wrote, called, and stopped by our fire-damaged offices this past week: thank you. Your concern for our staff and for this little paper carried us through a hard stretch.

Our offices sit just beside the Greymane Market hall, and when the fire took hold there, the building was evacuated at once for the safety of our staff. In the days since it has needed professional cleaning and a full inspection to be certain the structure was sound and the air was safe before anyone returned to work.

We are glad to report that the presses are running again and our people are safe and well. The Wexamour Chronicle is back in action, and regular publishing resumes with this compendium.

We could not be more grateful to be back in your hands.
