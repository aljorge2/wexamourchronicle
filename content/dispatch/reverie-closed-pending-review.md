The Office of Resource and Civil Management confirmed this week that the premises at the restored Misthollow lodge, operating as the entertainment establishment known as The Reverie, have been closed on a temporary basis pending an administrative review. The order took effect on receipt and remains in force until the review concludes.

An office spokesperson stated that the action was taken in the ordinary course following the receipt of a number of resident inquiries regarding persons said to be unaccounted for. The office noted that such inquiries do not constitute findings and that no determination has been made at this time. The premises are to remain closed to the public for the duration.

The office advised that it does not comment on matters under review, that the review will proceed according to standard procedure, and that further notices will be issued as and when appropriate. Members of the public with information relevant to the review are asked to direct it to the ORCM waystation during posted hours.
