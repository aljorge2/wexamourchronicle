If you are raising little ones anywhere in the Rolling Downs, pull up a chair. RollingMoms is the Commons channel for the parents of the Downs, from Millhaven to Brannock Crossing and every lane in between. This is the place to swap the things nobody prints in a parenting book: which fern meadow is gentlest for a first broom lesson, how to get honey cake out of a wool jumper, and what to do when a tuskboar advisory lands the same week as a birthday party.

Here is what this channel is for. Mom tips, the practical kind, traded freely with judgment left at the door. Finding and recommending babysitters and minders you actually trust, because the Downs has always run on knowing whose hands are safe. And advice, plain and simple, for the days when you have run clean out of your own.

A few ground rules so it stays the warm corner it is meant to be. Be kind; everyone here is tired. Recommend a sitter by name only with the family's blessing, and if you had a poor experience, say so gently and stick to what actually happened. No selling, that is what FadedMarketplace is for. And what gets shared here stays here.

Introduce yourself below. Tell us where in the Downs you are, how many little ones you are chasing, and the one piece of advice you wish someone had handed you on day one. Welcome to RollingMoms.
