If you are reading this, you are probably the same kind of person I am: someone who cannot let a strange story sit unexplained, and who would rather keep an eye on the neighborhood than wait to read about it later. That is what VaelthirWatch is. Part neighborhood watch, part case board for people who love a good mystery and hate a loose end. We pay attention to what happens around Vaelthir, especially the things the official record waves off in a sentence or two.

The idea is simple. Notice things, write them down, and put them together. Odd happenings, details that do not add up, public records, timelines, sightings, the small civic stuff nobody else bothers to track. Bring what you have and the rest of us will help you check it. Half of solving anything is just refusing to look away.

A few ground rules so we stay useful and stay welcome here. Verified information only; if you cannot source it, flag it as speculation and let the rest of us weigh in. No harassment of anyone, named or otherwise, because we are a watch, not a mob. Tag your posts so they thread to the right place. And look out for each other and for yourselves. If something feels off or unsafe, bring it here before you go poking at it alone.
