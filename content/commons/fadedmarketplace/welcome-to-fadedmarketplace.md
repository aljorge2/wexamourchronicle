If you have something to sell, something to find, or something you would part with for the right offer, this is the place. FadedMarketplace is the Commons channel for trade across the Faded Lands: goods, livestock, creature companions, curiosities, services, and the occasional thing that defies easy description. Post what you have or what you need and let the community do the rest.

A few rules to keep things running. Be honest about condition. Be honest about provenance. If an item has a history, say so, because things from the Faded Lands often do and buyers deserve to know what they are bringing home. No live creatures posted without a welfare note. No cursed items posted without a disclosure. If something goes wrong in a transaction, bring it here before you escalate elsewhere.

Tag your posts: FOR SALE, WANTED, TRADE, FREE TO GOOD HOME, or SERVICES. The last one is newer and growing, so use it.
