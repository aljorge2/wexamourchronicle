Welcome to VaelthirHour, the room that runs alongside the daily broadcast. If you have ever finished an episode of The Vaelthir Hour and thought someone should really be talking about this, that is what this channel is for.

This is the live desk. Breaking notes, corrections, and the small updates that move too fast for the morning edition get posted here through the day, so the Downs and the outer reaches hear it the moment we do. When a story is still developing, this is where it lives until it is solid enough to print.

It is also the pitch board. Tell us what you want covered on the daily podcast. A rumor that needs running down, a number that does not add up, a name that keeps coming up in the wrong rooms, or a bit of good news that deserves a segment of its own. Sorin reads this channel before every recording, and the strongest pitches go straight into the run of show.

A few ground rules so it stays useful. Bring a source, or say plainly that you cannot. Mark rumor as rumor. Keep it about the news and the show, not about the neighbors. And if we cover your pitch on air, we will say where it came from.

Pull up a chair and tell us what the Hour should be talking about tomorrow.
