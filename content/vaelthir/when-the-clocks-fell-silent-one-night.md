The McGuire Group, whose work has graced the stages of Wexamour since 500 P.M., returns this June with a single performance of their landmark production <em style="color: var(--gold);">When The Clocks Fell Silent</em>, playing Tuesday the 23rd at the Nascium Playhouse, 8878 Clairemont Mesa Blvd. Doors open at the eighth hour. For those attending, the Midnight Combo (two tickets, popcorn, and a confection of your choosing) is available for 4 to 4 and a half dollars. The Chronicle does not expect tickets to last.
