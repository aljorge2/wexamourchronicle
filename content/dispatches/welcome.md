Deep within the vaulted archives of the Wexamour Society, a gaslight was relit and a quill dipped once more into ink darker than midnight. After a silence that stretched across seasons uncounted, *The Wexamour Chronicle* resumes its solemn duty: to illuminate what history prefers to leave in shadow.

We make no apologies for our long absence. The truth is a patient hunter.

In the issues to come, our correspondents will lay bare the myths dressed as fact, the theatre dressed as history, and the hidden currents beneath the great events that shaped this world. We invite readers of discerning taste — those who look upon the official record not with reverence, but with a raised eyebrow — to join us.

The Chronicle does not sensationalize. We do not fabricate. We *excavate*.

Welcome back to the light.
