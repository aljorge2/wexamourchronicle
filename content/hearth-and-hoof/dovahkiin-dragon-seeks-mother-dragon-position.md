Dovahkiin, seventeen, Ashwing classification, is formally seeking placement as a Mother Dragon with a private clutch, institutional hatchery, or legacy brood in need of experienced maternal oversight. He has warmed forty-one clutches over the course of his career, supervised eight full hatchling cohorts through their first flight assessments, and holds a current certification in emergency shell triage from the University of Vaelthir. His flame is a stable amber-gold, well-suited to sustained incubation temperatures, and he has maintained a fire suppression protocol since 5740 P.M. that his previous employers describe as "genuinely reassuring" and "a significant improvement over the earlier years." Dovahkiin is calm under pressure, patient with slow hatchers, and firm but fair with hatchlings who attempt to establish dominance before they have grown their second scale row. He works well independently but also respects chain-of-command structures in larger hatchery environments. It should be noted that his maternal experience extends well beyond traditional dragon clutches: he has successfully brought four-hundred-pound manticores to full maturity, a detail his references are prepared to confirm and which he considers some of his finest work. He is available for full-season commitments, emergency clutch coverage, or long-term household placement with a breeding pair who require dedicated maternal support. He does not require a treasure arrangement, only adequate cave temperature, weekly access to mountain thermals, and what he describes as "a reasonable amount of professional respect." Dovahkiin is not seeking positions involving snow clutches, as he has strong feelings about snow clutches. Interested parties may reach out to the Vaelthir Draconic Placement Bureau by owl.
