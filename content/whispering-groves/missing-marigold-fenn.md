The Chronicle is asking for the public's help in finding one of its own, and does so at the express wish of her family, who want her named.

<strong style="color: var(--gold);">Name:</strong> Marigold Fenn, health and wellness correspondent for these pages and a recent arrival to Wexamour.

<strong style="color: var(--gold);">Last seen:</strong> more than three weeks ago, after telling the editors she meant to travel out to the restored lodge off the Misthollow fire road to report a second piece on The Reverie.

<strong style="color: var(--gold);">Description:</strong> slight of build, in the habit of making the forty minute broom run from Vaelthir into the Groves. Her rooms in Vaelthir are as she left them, and she has answered no owl since.

<strong style="color: var(--gold);">If you have seen her:</strong> write to the Whispering Groves desk by owl. Any word at all, however small, will be carried straight to her family.

We would not ordinarily print a colleague's name in a matter like this. We print it now because those closest to her asked us to, in the hope that someone who passed her on the fire road, or stood beside her in the queue at the lodge, will remember, and tell us.
